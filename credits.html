• Hyrac Box Resources Credits

    HyracTech, is a social tech company, whose vision is to impact the current underserved generation in the developing world through the provision of knowledge and skills that are essential for thriving in this digital era.

    Hyrac Box on the other hand is an offline mobile server designed to bring internet resources offline.

    We recognize and appreciate other initiaves and organizations that have done a tremendous job over the years to bridge the digital divide by curating and providing digital content & applications focusing on education, health care, personal development and much more.

    The Hyrac Box makes use of many open source resources attributed to the organizations in this page.

  • Internet-in-a-Box (formerly known as XSCE School Server)

    This platform is built on top of Internet-in-a-Box. The amazing team at IIAB have been very instrumental in guiding us from the very beginning. Our engineering team works closely with IIAB to contribute to the open source project and share ideas on our vision of bridging the digital divide.

    Internet-in-a-Box distribution resides at github.com/iiab.
